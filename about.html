What is Sounds of Summer

Sounds of Summer is a week long summer camp where students will be exposed to the wonders of classical music. Students will be introduced to classical instruments such as the violin, viola, trumpet, cello, and piano. Students will also learn about the history of classical music by learning about classical composers such as Johann Sebastion Bach, Ludwig van Beethoven, and George Fridiric Handel.

Why learn classical music

Cultural enrichment: is an integral part of human culture and history. By introducing students to this artform at a young age, we can help them appreciate and understand cultural heritage and traditions.

Our purpose

In the 21st century, our students lack education that teaches classical music. At Sounds of Summer, our goal is to introduce young students to the world of classical music.
